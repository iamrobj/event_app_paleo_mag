There has never been a better time to be a foodie in London

London has undoubtedly earned itself as one of the biggest food capitals in the world. Soggy chips and suspect burgers are a thing of the past and top chefs, food trends and cuisines have flocked to the capital. With street food, more food trucks and pop up’s than you can shake a stick at, supper clubs, food markets & food festivals to be explored. Hunting down new food experiences is now essentially a hobby and if that’s wrong, then we don’t want to be right.

London has proved itself to be a foodie’s heaven and this is the ultimate guide so you don’t miss out...
So go forth, and get stuff’d!

Foodies Festival Blackheath,
June 30

The Foodies Festival comes to Blackheath again this summer for three days of culinary fun. The UK's biggest celebration of food and drink, Foodies is known for its packed programmes and this festival will be no different with chefs such as Rosemary Shrager, Adam Simmonds and Jane Devonshire on the line-up.

7th - 9th July

Join Fortnum & Mason for a once-in-a-lifetime candlelit midnight feast in our famous food halls, when a special menu of exceptional Fortnum’s food and drink will be served, including Glenarm beef from our butcher, salmon from our fish counter, finest cheeses and more.
